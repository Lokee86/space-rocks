# Achievements And Milestones

## Purpose

This doc plans the achievement and milestone seam for long-lived player goals and one-time or tiered recognition.

## Ownership Boundary

This doc owns planning for achievement definitions, trusted fact inputs, one-time and tiered milestones, unlock grants, and reward interaction.

It should stay separate from broader progression policy while still feeding into it.

## Current Inputs

- achievement definitions
- trusted fact inputs
- one-time milestone inputs
- tiered milestone inputs
- unlock grant inputs
- reward interaction inputs

## Planned Outputs

- achievement and milestone ownership boundaries
- trusted fact requirements for progress tracking
- the handoff shape into unlock and reward systems
