# Matchmaking And Room Discovery
Parent index: [Platform Planning](../!INDEX.md)

## Purpose

This doc plans the matchmaking and room-discovery seam for getting players into the right room.

## Ownership Boundary

This doc owns planning for matchmaking, room discovery metadata, hosted multiplayer needs, and the relationship to lobby and room creation.

It should stay focused on discovery and entry flow rather than simulation or match-rule policy.

- room creation inputs

## Planned Outputs

- matchmaking and discovery boundaries
- room metadata expectations
- the handoff between lobby flow and room creation
