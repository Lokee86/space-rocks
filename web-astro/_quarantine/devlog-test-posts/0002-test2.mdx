intro: |
  Space Rocks! started from the arcade fantasy: ship, rocks, thrust, shooting, survival.

  Inspired by the classic that we all know and love, that basic loop is still the center of the project. But shooting floating rocks in space is where the similarities end.

  Space Rocks! has grown into a multiplayer space shooter concept, with persistent pilots, match flow, player accounts, stats, rooms, matchmaking, and the early foundation for much more expansive gameplay. Longer-term plans include campaign content, hectic PvP combat, and durable progression, all without losing that nostalgic arcade feeling.

  Those added systems serve to enhance the traditional chaos with modern structure and progression: providing a persistent identity, altering how each match unfolds, keeping track of what you’ve done, and setting up what comes next.
